Traffic (blinking orange light) Roundabouts
On multilane roundabouts (turbo-roundabouts), it is important to choose the correct lane. After that, just keep following your lane and you will end up at the correct place.

When leaving the roundabout, you often (but not always) need to give way to bicyclists and pedestrians. Pay attention to the shark teeth and zebra crossing. Speed limits Speed limits announced by such signs are valid until the next intersection.
Within towns, the speed limit is 50km/h.
Outside of towns, the speed limit is 80km/h.
On autowegen, the speed limit is 130km/h.
